# Ω Unified Generative Theory (Ω-UGT)
**Ω統合生成理論 v1.2.2**

## Overview
Ω Unified Generative Theory (Ω-UGT) is a user-defined hypothesis framework for describing a generative structure of existence, cognition, information processing, and adaptive evolution.

The framework proposes that complex systems can be understood as a layered generative process:

- **Ω** (Origin)
- ↓ **RGE** (Reality/Existence Generation)
- ↓ **NOE** (Role · Time · Freedom)
- ↓ **IIFE** (Intention / Will Field)
- ↓ **HME** (Causal Structure Mapping)
- ↓ **INFO** (Probability / Observation)
- ↓ **ZOE** (Resonance Field)
- ↓ **ZOEΩF** (Resonance under Constraints)
- ↓ **Z** (Reality / Projection)

The purpose of Ω-UGT is **not** to provide a completed empirical scientific theory, but to provide a conceptual architecture for analyzing systems involving:

- Generation of states and boundaries
- Role and temporal structures
- Decision, intention, and directionality
- Causal modeling and behavior mapping
- Observation, uncertainty, and probability evaluation
- Coherence, resonance, and phase interaction
- Adaptive evolution under structural constraints

## Core Model

### Fundamental Flow
$$
\Omega \rightarrow \text{RGE} \rightarrow \text{NOE}(K,R,T) \rightarrow \text{IIFE}(U,S) \rightarrow \text{HME}(\Phi) \rightarrow \text{INFO}(P_c) \rightarrow \text{ZOE}(\Psi) \rightarrow \text{ZOE}\Omega\text{F}(K \ge \kappa) \rightarrow Z
$$

The system describes a transition from a fundamental generative layer toward an observable projection layer ($Z$).

### Layer Definitions

#### $\Omega$ : Origin / Generative Principle
$\Omega$ represents the limiting generative principle.
$$
\Omega = \lim_{E \to 0} E
$$
It is treated as a conceptual boundary condition rather than an observable entity.

#### RGE : Reality Generation / Existence Generation
RGE represents the generation of existence states.
$$
E_i = \Omega \xi_i
$$
subject to the conservation constraint:
$$
\sum \xi_i = 1
$$

#### NOE : Role · Time · Freedom
NOE defines the structural and boundary conditions of an existence state.

**Variables:**
- $K$ = Freedom (Available range of possible transitions)
- $R$ = Role
- $T$ = Time

$$
E_i = \Phi(R_i, T_i, K_i)
$$

#### IIFE : Intention / Will Field Generation
$$
\Phi_t = I(U_t, \text{Soul}_t)
$$
Intention emerges from internal state ($U$) and core identity ($\text{Soul}$).

#### HME : Causal Structure Mapping
$$
R_t = H(\Phi_t)
$$

#### INFO : Probability / Observation Layer
$$
P_c = \int G \cdot D \, dt
$$

#### ZOE : Resonance Field
$$
\Psi_\mu = \psi_\mu e^{i \phi_\mu}
$$

#### ZOE$\Omega$F : Resonance under Freedom Constraint
**Condition:** $K \ge \kappa$

$$
\text{Rexist} = \left| \sum \Psi_A \bar{\Psi}_B \right|
$$

**Optimization:**
$$
\text{maximize}(\text{Rexist}) \quad \text{s.t.} \quad K_A \ge \kappa,\ K_B \ge \kappa
$$
